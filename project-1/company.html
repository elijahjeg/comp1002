About Sony Corporation

Innovating Entertainment Since 1946

Sony Corporation, founded in 1946 in Tokyo, Japan, has been a global leader in electronics, entertainment, and gaming for decades. With a commitment to innovation and quality, Sony has introduced groundbreaking products like the Walkman, PlayStation, and Bravia TVs.

Beyond electronics, Sony is also a major player in the music and film industries through its subsidiaries Sony Music Entertainment and Sony Pictures Entertainment. The company's mission is to inspire and fulfill curiosity through technology and creativity.

With a presence in over 190 countries, Sony continues to shape the future of entertainment and technology, delivering exceptional experiences to customers worldwide.

Fun Fact

The first Walkman was called the “TPS-L2” and only available in Japan in 1979.
